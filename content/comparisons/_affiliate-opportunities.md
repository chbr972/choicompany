## Detailed Program Information

### Grammarly
- **Platform:** Impact.com
- **Commission:** $20 per Premium subscription (or $0.20 per free signup, $20 per premium)
- **Cookie:** 90 days
- **Conversion rate:** High — Grammarly is a household name with strong brand trust
- **Payout threshold:** $50
- **Best for:** Grammarly vs. ChatGPT, Grammarly review pages, writing tool roundups
- **Application:** Direct application at grammarly.com/affiliates

### Jasper AI
- **Platform:** PartnerStack
- **Commission:** 30% recurring for the lifetime of the customer
- **Cookie:** 60 days
- **Average customer LTV:** High (>$500 typical annual plan)
- **Commission example:** $49/month × 30% = $14.70/month per customer indefinitely
- **Best for:** Jasper vs. Copy.ai, Jasper vs. ChatGPT, AI writing tool roundups
- **Application:** partners.jasper.ai

### Copy.ai
- **Platform:** Direct (affiliate.copy.ai)
- **Commission:** 45% recurring commission for 12 months
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Commission example:** $49/month × 45% = $22.05/month for 12 months = $264.60 per customer
- **Note:** Higher rate than Jasper but time-limited (12 months vs. lifetime)
- **Best for:** Copy.ai vs. Jasper, Copy.ai vs. ChatGPT comparison pages
- **Application:** affiliate.copy.ai

### Adobe Creative Cloud (Firefly)
- **Platform:** CJ Affiliate (Commission Junction)
- **Commission:** 8.33% of first month purchase OR fixed bounty (~$85.99 per CC All Apps signup)
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Average order value:** $59.99/month CC All Apps
- **Note:** Adobe has strict affiliate quality requirements — content must be professional
- **Best for:** Midjourney vs. Adobe Firefly, AI image generation roundups
- **Application:** adobe.com/affiliates

### ClickUp
- **Platform:** PartnerStack
- **Commission:** $50 per Business plan referral
- **Cookie:** 90 days
- **Note:** B2B product with higher conversion value per signup
- **Best for:** ClickUp AI comparison pages, project management tool roundups
- **Application:** clickup.com/affiliates

### Perplexity AI
- **Platform:** Impact.com
- **Commission:** 20% recurring commission for 12 months
- **Cookie:** 30 days
- **Commission example:** $20/month × 20% = $4/month for 12 months = $48 per customer
- **Note:** Growing program — early mover advantage
- **Best for:** ChatGPT vs. Perplexity, Claude vs. Perplexity comparison pages
- **Application:** perplexity.ai/affiliate

### Notion
- **Type:** Referral program (credits, not cash)
- **Reward:** $10 in Notion credit per successful referral
- **Note:** Not a traditional cash affiliate program — better suited as a secondary CTA
- **Best for:** Notion AI comparison pages as a secondary conversion goal

## Affiliate Strategy Recommendations

### Highest Revenue Priority Pages
Based on traffic × conversion potential:

1. **Grammarly vs. ChatGPT** — Grammarly $20/signup × high search volume = top affiliate earner
2. **Jasper vs. Copy.ai** — Both tools pay; double affiliate opportunity per page
3. **Jasper vs. ChatGPT** — Jasper's 30% lifetime commission on $49+ plans
4. **Copy.ai vs. ChatGPT** — Copy.ai's 45% commission is the highest rate in the category
5. **ClickUp AI comparisons** — $50 B2B bounty with 90-day cookie

### Implementation Notes

**Disclosure requirement:** All comparison pages with affiliate links must include a disclosure notice at the bottom. Current pages include: *"Affiliate disclosure: This comparison contains affiliate links. See our [disclosure policy](/about/affiliate-disclosure)."*

**Link placement best practices:**
- Primary affiliate link: in the "Which Should You Choose?" section
- Secondary: in pricing tables (link tool names to affiliate URLs)
- CTA buttons: "Try [Tool Name] Free →" convert better than text links

**Tracking:**
- Use UTM parameters on all affiliate links: `?utm_source=comparison&utm_medium=affiliate&utm_campaign=[comparison-slug]`
- Set up conversion tracking in Google Analytics to measure which comparison pages drive affiliate revenue

### Tools Without Affiliate Programs

For comparisons involving ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, GitHub Copilot, and Stable Diffusion (no affiliate programs):
- Monetize primarily through display advertising (these pages still attract high-CPM developer/AI audiences)
- Use non-affiliate alternatives: link to Amazon books on prompt engineering, Udemy courses on AI tools, or other adjacent affiliate products
- Consider adding affiliate links to *related* tools (e.g., on ChatGPT vs. Claude, link to Grammarly for "enhance your AI-generated content")
