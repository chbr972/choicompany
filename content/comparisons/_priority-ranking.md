## Traffic Optimization Notes

### Title SEO Priorities
- Lead with the most popular tool in the comparison (e.g., "ChatGPT vs. Claude" not "Claude vs. ChatGPT")
- Include the year in the title (2026) — improves CTR in search results
- Target "vs." comparisons: these keywords have high commercial intent

### Content Freshness
- Tier 1 comparisons should be updated quarterly — pricing and feature changes are frequent
- Set up a content calendar to refresh the top 5 comparisons every 3 months
- Monitor pricing changes for all tools — outdated pricing damages credibility

### Internal Linking Strategy
- Each comparison page should link to at least 2 related comparisons
- Add "Also see" sections at the bottom of each comparison
- Build a /compare/ hub page that links to all 20 comparisons

### Ad Placement Recommendations
High-CPM placement for comparison pages:
- Above the fold: mid-article banner (avoid blocking the overview table)
- In-content: after each major section (every 3-4 H2 sections)
- Sidebar: sticky ad for desktop readers
- Bottom: before the verdict section (high-intent readers)
